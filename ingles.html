¿What are Biogas?

Biogas is the only renewable energy that can be used for any of the major energy applications: electrical, thermal or as fuel.
Biogas is a type of renewable gas that is obtained from organic waste from industries such as food, agriculture or livestock.
Basically, wherever there is biodegradable organic matter (that decomposes) it is possible to obtain this advanced biofuel.
Biogas is obtained from organic waste, since biodegradation of plant residues similar to that described occurs in them.
The production of biogas by anaerobic decomposition is a useful way of treating biodegradable waste, as it produces a valuable fuel,
in addition to generating an effluent that can be applied as a soil conditioner or generic fertilizer.

Biodogestors

A biodigester is a container or tank (hermetically closed) that is loaded with organic waste. Inside, the decomposition of organic matter occurs
to generate biogas, a fuel with which you can cook, heat water and produce electrical energy, using a gas generator.
The main function of the biodigester is precisely this, to treat the wastewater so that when it is expelled into the environment it causes the minimum
